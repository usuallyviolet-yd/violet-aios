# AI Evaluation

## Simple Definition

AI evaluation is the practice of testing whether an AI system produces outputs that meet defined quality, safety, reliability, and business requirements.

## Human Analogy

Evaluation is the quality-control system for AI products.

## Why Traditional Testing Is Not Enough

Generative AI can produce different outputs for similar inputs. That makes normal software testing insufficient. AI systems need evals that check quality across many examples and edge cases.

## Common Evaluation Types

- accuracy
- relevance
- groundedness
- hallucination rate
- instruction following
- safety/compliance
- tool-use success
- latency and cost
- user satisfaction
- business outcome impact

## Evaluation Methods

- human review
- golden test sets
- LLM-as-judge
- unit tests for structured outputs
- regression tests before model/prompt changes
- production monitoring
- task completion metrics

## Product Lens

An AI product without evals is not production-ready. Evals help teams decide whether to launch, rollback, improve prompts, change models, add guardrails, or redesign the workflow.

## Violet Connection

Evaluation is a major differentiator for AI product leadership. It connects product quality, user trust, engineering reliability, and GTM claims.
