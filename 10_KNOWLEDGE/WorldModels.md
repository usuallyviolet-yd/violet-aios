# World Models

## Simple Definition

A world model is an AI system that learns how an environment works, including how it changes over time and how actions affect it.

## Human Analogy

A language model predicts text. A world model tries to predict the behavior of a world.

## Why It Matters

World models are important because many real problems require understanding space, time, physics, interaction, and causality — not just text.

## Product Lens

World models could change how AI interacts with physical and virtual environments. Instead of only describing a building, a future AI system may understand, simulate, modify, and reason about it.
